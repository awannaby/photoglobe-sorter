PhotoGlobe Sorter: The Complete Software to Sort and Organize Your Photos Looking for a solution to organize photos on your computer? PhotoGlobe Sorter is the automatic photo sorting software that analyzes hidden EXIF metadata in your files to create smart organization by date and location. Unlike cloud solutions that store your photos on external servers, PhotoGlobe Sorter runs 100% locally: your data stays on your hard drive, guaranteeing total privacy. Our software to sort photos by date and location uses GPS geolocation embedded in EXIF metadata to automatically identify where each photo was taken. The system translates place names into over 50 languages, creating a unified world atlas in English. Provided you activate the feature with your (free) MapTiler API key, you can see your photos on maps with heat maps showing your most frequent photography locations. PhotoGlobe Sorter is offered as a $39 lifetime license, with no monthly or annual subscription. It's a no-subscription software you buy once and use forever. The free trial lets you test all features on 500 photos before buying. Compatible with Windows 10/11; macOS 10.15+ and Linux (Ubuntu, Debian, Fedora) versions are planned for 2026. Whether you're looking for free software to organize your photos (trial version), a tool to sort family or vacation photos, or a professional photo library organization solution, PhotoGlobe Sorter meets all needs. The drag-and-drop interface makes sorting photos into folders simple and intuitive, even for beginners. PhotoGlobe Sorter supports only the following photo formats: JPG/JPEG, PNG, TIFF. The software automatically extracts EXIF metadata from all these formats. PhotoGlobe Sorter does not support the following photo formats: RAW (CR2, NEF, ARW, DNG, etc.), BMP, and WebP.
