The Process: Sorting 8 Years of Weekends in 25 Minutes

Bought the licence. Paid with my card, the €39 came out as £34.20 on my statement. Easy peasy. Here's how I sorted my 15,000+ travel photos.

Step 1: The "Euro Explorer" Structure (5 minutes)

This was dead simple. In the "Structure Builder", I set up my ideal travel log:

Country → City → Year → Month

Perfect. The software would read the GPS tag from my phone pics and automatically create folders like `France > Paris > 2024 > 05_May`. Genius!

Step 2: Old Camera Pics (Maybe 30 minutes)

My old mirrorless camera didn't have GPS. So, about 1,000 photos were "Location Unknown". The software has a manual geotagging tool. I just selected my old "Berlin_2018" photos, clicked Berlin on the map, and job done. Did this for a few old trips while watching the telly.

Step 3: Hit 'Go' (25 Minutes)

I pointed the "Source" to my messy main photo folder. Pointed the "Destination" to a new folder on my external drive. Clicked "Start Sorting".

Total time for 15,800 photos: 25 minutes flat. Blimey, that was quick!

Step 4: The Result (Tidy Folders & Brilliant Stats!)

At the end, it popped up the "Sort Summary" page. And it was brilliant!
